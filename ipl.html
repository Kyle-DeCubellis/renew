Correct pigment, redness, and sun damage

IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) therapy is one of the most effective non-invasive treatments for correcting uneven skin tone, sun spots, broken capillaries, and rosacea.

Using broad-spectrum light energy, IPL targets pigment and vascular irregularities beneath the skin's surface — stimulating the body's natural healing response for clearer, more even skin. Treatments are quick, with little to no downtime, and results build beautifully over a series of sessions.

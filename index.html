About Us

Medicine.
Artistry.
Wellness.

Founded in 2018 by Dr. Teri Ciccone and Beth Domolky, RN, Renew Aesthetic Medicine & Wellness has grown into a trusted destination for aesthetic medicine and total-body wellness in Southborough, MA.

Our team of expert clinicians combines the science of medicine with an artist's eye — offering everything from Botox and fillers to medical weight loss, hormone therapy, and advanced skin treatments.

We believe in treating the whole patient. Your goals, your comfort, and your results drive every decision we make.

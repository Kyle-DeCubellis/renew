Resurface and renew your skin's texture

Microneedling harnesses your skin's own regenerative power — using controlled micro-channels to trigger collagen and elastin production for visibly smoother, firmer, more radiant skin.

At Renew, microneedling is performed by our expert esthetician Melanie Carvalho using medical-grade devices. The treatment is effective for fine lines, acne scars, enlarged pores, uneven texture, and general skin rejuvenation. Optional add-ons like PRP or hyaluronic acid serums can amplify results. Most patients see significant improvement after just a few sessions.
